The lives of women trapped in the pink tax

1. What is pink tax

1.1 video

1.2 illustrate

In 1991, Lan Ayres, an American economist and jurist, coined the term to refer to the practice of differential pricing of goods and services based on gender. The essence of the pink tax is price discrimination. First, women pay extra for different packages of the same product.The second layer is an additional price for feminine products.

2. The manifestation of America's pink tax

The concept of the "pink tax" originated in the United States, and the price difference caused by gender is 7% higher for women than for men.

2.1 Product price discrimination in the United States

As can be seen from the chart, the price of every type of product for women is higher than that for men, and the pink tax quietly sweeps the price of all kinds of commodities like an invisible wind.

2.2 Which products contain the pink tax?

You can see that eight product categories, from children's clothing to dry cleaning, all contain a "pink tax", and even dry cleaning products contain a pink tax of 64%.

2.3 Cumulative pink tax

The "pink tax" is not only for a young female group, in fact, from birth to the grave, women are not surrounded by the "pink tax" for a moment.

3. China's "pink tax" situation

In China, although the discussion of the "pink tax" has only emerged in recent years, it does not prevent it from already silently existing in the market, which is a trap carefully woven by businesses for women.

3.1 Different colors of the same product price difference

The statistical results show that in the same model of goods, compared with the average price of male stereotyped colors such as black, blue and gray, female stereotyped colors such as pink, purple and beige are at a premium.

3.2 The price difference between men and women of the same type of product

Not only different colors of the same product, but also hidden pink taxes between the same type of product with the same function. At the same time, pink tax is not limited to product pricing discrimination against stereotypical colors and gender styles, product and service differentiation is also an important embodiment of pink tax.

3.3 Women's product

Today, the concept of a pink tax has been extended to a wider range of areas. Products with additional properties such as menstrual use and menstrual cramps will be more expensive than ordinary models with the same efficacy. Products with women's keywords have no special ingredients added, but are just made up on the packaging.

4. Dissenting voices

"Is it wrong to like beautiful things?"

"Is it wrong to like pink colors?"

In fact, as the idea of women changes More and more people began to notice and Resist this unreasonable premium on products.

5. Audience attitude

According to data from Baidu Consulting Index, most people will realize that women's products will be more expensive than men's products during the consumption process.

5.1 How to understand the pink tax?

We counted the comments on Bilibili's 3 most popular videos introducing the pink tax, totaling 4,182. The most common idea is that the pink tax is a product of women's socially disciplined preferences. There are also a lot of voices that this is just the normal operation of the profit-seeking market, and supply and demand affect prices.

5.2 Why are you resisting the pink tax?

And the girls are fighting back. Douban group "Pink Tax Boycott Alliance", members through the community to share the pink tax products of the weeding and demining experience, to warn each other not to be cut by the pink tax leek.

They tell us that girls refuse to pay the pink tax not only because it is really comfortable, cheap, convenient and easy to dress, but also in order to oppose the social stereotypes of women, and at least from their own starting to make a little effort for gender equality and freedom.

6. Conclusion

A gender-based "pink tax" is one The embodiment of gender discrimination, by attaching gender attributes, solid Gender labels to raise the price of products, is a kind of market It was a mess. Merchants are prohibited from using this kind of consumer trap At the same time, female consumers should also raise their awareness Knowledge, avoid falling into the trap of merchants.
